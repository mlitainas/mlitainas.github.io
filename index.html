About Me

I am an applied macroeconomist with a PhD in Economics and experience across Government, Central Banking, Academia, and Policy Institutions. My research focuses on international economics, fiscal and monetary policy, and energy shocks. I specialize in time-series econometrics and general equilibrium models, with proficiency in R, Matlab, and Python.
