Welcome to my website!

I am an applied Macroeconomist, with strong interest in data analysis and empirical research methods. My work has covered a range of topics in the areas of international economics, fiscal and monetary policy and oil shocks. I have a solid background in time-series and panel data econometrics, along with advanced programming skills in several programming languages.
